Cheesy Tacos

Cheesy Tacos are a delectable Tex-Mex dish that combines the satisfying crunch of a crispy tortilla shell with a savory filling of seasoned ground beef, melted cheddar cheese, and an array of flavorful toppings. These handheld delights are perfect for casual gatherings, family dinners, or any occasion where you want to savor a mouthwatering blend of cheesy goodness and zesty flavors. Indulge in the cheesy taco experience and let your taste buds rejoice!

Ingredients

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.
  3. Place a spoonful of the cooked ground beef onto each tortilla.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese, diced tomatoes, diced onions, and chopped cilantro over the ground beef.
  5. Drizzle sour cream and salsa on top of the other ingredients.
  6. Roll up the tortillas tightly, securing the fillings inside.
  7. Optional: If desired, heat the assembled tacos on a skillet to melt the cheese and lightly toast the tortillas.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
