Mexican Fries

Mexican Fries are a mouthwatering twist on traditional french fries, infused with bold and zesty Mexican flavors. These crispy potato delights are seasoned with a blend of chili powder, garlic, and cumin, then topped with melted cheddar cheese, fresh cilantro, diced tomatoes, onions, sour cream, and salsa for an explosion of taste. Indulge in this irresistible fusion of American comfort food and Mexican cuisine that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and dry the potatoes, then cut them into fries or wedges.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the potato pieces with olive oil, chili powder, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  4. Spread the seasoned potato pieces in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the fries are golden brown and crispy, flipping them halfway through.
  6. Remove the fries from the oven and sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the hot fries to allow it to melt.
  7. Top the cheesy fries with chopped cilantro, diced tomatoes, diced onions, sour cream, and salsa.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Mexican-inspired fries!
